Book description

Animal Planet Ocean species is a fun, habitat-by-habitat guide that provides kids in the first years of school with the perfect bite-sized perspective of their favorite ocean-dwelling species, with over 200 magnificent animal photographs of sharks, whales, clown fish, jelly fish, dolphins, and more. Young readers will discover sections on animal bodies, baby animals, food, play time, conservation, and more, which are organized thematically with a focus on animal behavior and family relationships. Quick Bites sidebars with intriguing animal information, basic infographics, and illustrated maps of Earth's watery ecosystems are special book elements created for this age group.

For more Animal Bites books, check out Animal Planet Farm Animals, Animal Planet Polar Animals, and Animal Planet Ocean Animals. A portion of the proceeds will benefit Animal Planet's R.O.A.R. (Reach Out. Act. Respond.) campaign that partners with leading animal organization to make the world a better place for domestic and wild animals.
